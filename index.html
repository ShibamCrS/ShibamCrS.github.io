Current Project

Currently, as a part of the program I am working on my masters thesis at Inria, Paris under the supervision of Anne CANTEAUT and Leo PERRIN.

My current project in Inria is on Properties of quadratic APN and crooked functions. Main focus of our project is to study of the properties of APN functions of degree 2 in order to find new families of such functions which are in different CCZ-equivalence class.
